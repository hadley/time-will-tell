## Description

Keep your talks on track with a timer designed for speakers and session chairs.

Time Will Tell displays a bold countdown visible from across the room. As time runs out, the screen changes color to give you clear visual warnings without interrupting your flow:

• Black: You're in the safe zone
• Yellow: Time to start wrapping up
• Red: Finish your final point
• Flashing: Time's up STOP TALKING

Perfect for:
• Conference presentations
• Academic talks and defenses
• Chairing sessions with multiple speakers
• Lectures and seminars
• Lightning talks and unconferences

Features:
• Large, readable display optimized for distance viewing
• Customizable talk duration (1-120 minutes)
• Adjustable warning thresholds
• Haptic alerts when entering warning zones
• Scrubber bar for quick time adjustments
• Screen stays awake during countdown
• Landscape orientation for maximum visibility

Prop your phone on the lectern, set your time, and focus on what matters—your talk.
